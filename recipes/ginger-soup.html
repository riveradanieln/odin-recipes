Ginger Soup

Description

The idea behind this meal was that I was feeling deathly ill and I needed somthing somewhat hot that could be thrown together. I was craving ginger at the time so this is what came of that. The key to this recipe is throwing any amount of ingredients into a pot of water and add salt.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Ginger
  • Green onion
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Roughly chop all the ingredients and add them to a pot to sautee
  2. Cover with water and simmer for at least 20 minutes
  3. Optional: Strain the soup

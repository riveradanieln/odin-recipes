Flan

Description

This is one of my all time favorite desserts. Not only is it rich, it's easy to make *recipe from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Melt sugar in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat until liquefied and golden in color. Carefully pour hot syrup into a deep 9 inch round glass baking dish, turning the dish evenly to coat the bottom; set aside.
  3. Beat eggs in a large bowl and add the remaining liquid ingredients.
  4. Pour egg micture in the baking dish and place in a deep pan with enough water to come 1 inch up the sides of the dish
  5. Bake until just set with a slight jiggle in the center (about 1hr)
  6. Let cool then invert the dessert on a rimmed serving plate
